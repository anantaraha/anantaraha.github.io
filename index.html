I am Ananta Raha, a CSE graduate with a strong research interest spanning Computer Vision, Medical Imaging, and Explainable AI. Particularly, I am motivated by developing trustworthy AI methods that ensure interpretability, efficiency, and privacy.

Following my undergraduate thesis on computationally-efficient brain tumor segmentation (from MRI), I have researched lightweight architectures for medical diagnosis and precision agriculture. During my graduate studies, I worked on several independent and team projects in diverse areas, such as inexpensive sericulture, neural network privacy, NGS read disambiguation, and human-computer interaction. These experiences have reinforced my research perspectives and implementation skills.

I am actively seeking Ph.D. opportunities that will allow me to extend my foundation in lightweight vision toward impactful research in trustworthy healthcare and agriculture.
